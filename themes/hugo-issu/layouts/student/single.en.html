*1

Excluding specialized courses and teaching courses in the fourth semester of the College of Liberal Arts.

*2

In order to take the Special Seminar in Information Science in the fourth grade, students must have earned at least 10 credits out of the 12 credits required in the fourth semester of the second grade in advance.

*3

In order to take the Special Seminar in Information Science in the fourth grade, students must have earned at least 24 credits out of the 32 credits required in the third grade, including System Programming Experiments, Functional and Logic Programming Experiments, Hardware Experiments, and Processor and Compiler Experiments.
