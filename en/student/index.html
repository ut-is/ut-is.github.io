Class Schedule

（Fiscal Year 2022）

For class dates and more information, please see the 2022 School of Science Schedule of Classes (PDF).

This is the class schedule for the Faculty of Science.
Please note that the schedule is different from that of the fourth semester of the first semester of the College of Liberal Arts and the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology.

Summer

S Semester : Monday, April 4 - Thursday, July 28

Examination and make-up class period : Wednesday, April 20 - Thursday, July 28

S1 Term : Monday, April 4 - Thursday, June 2

Examination and make-up class period : Tuesday, May 31 - Thursday, June 2

S2 Term : Friday, June 3 - Thursday, July 28

Examination and make-up class period : July 25 (Mon.) - July 28 (Thu.)

Summer Break : July 29 (Fri.) - September 30 (Fri.)

Classes are suspended for the entire day on Tuesday, April 12 (The University of Tokyo Commemoration Day (Entrance Ceremony))

Afternoon classes will be cancelled on May 9 (Mon.) (Faculty of Science Welcome Party (Koishikawa Botanical Garden)) [Tentative schedule].

May 13 (Fri.): Classes will be cancelled for the whole day (May Festival (Preparation))

Classes will be held all day on April 25 (Mon.) and on Friday.

Classes will be held all day on April 28 (Thursday) and all day on Tuesday.

On Monday, May 30, all classes will be held on Thursday.

On Tuesday, July 19, all classes will be held on Mondays.

On Friday, July 22, all classes will be held on Tuesday. (S2 Term Courses)

Registration period (S semester, S1 term, S2 term): April 4 (Mon.) - April 19 (Tue.)

Winter

Semester A : October 3 (Monday) - January 30 (Monday)

Examination and make-up class period : Tuesday, January 17 - Monday, January 30

A1 Term : Monday, October 3 - Monday, November 28

Examination and make-up class period : Monday, November 21 - Monday, November 28

A2 Term : Tuesday, November 29 - Monday, January 30

Examination and make-up class period : Tuesday, January 24 - Thursday, January 26

Winter Break : December 28 (Wed.) - January 3 (Tue.)

Spring Break : Tuesday, January 31 - Friday, March 31

Classes will be suspended for the whole day on November 18 (Fri.) (Komaba Festival)

Classes will be suspended on all days on January 13th (Fri.) (Common Test Preparation)

Registration period (A semester, A1 term, A2 term): October 3 (Mon.) - October 14 (Fri.)
