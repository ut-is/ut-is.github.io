Class Schedule

（Fiscal Year 2023）

For class dates and more information, please see the 2023 School of Science Schedule of Classes (PDF).

This is the class schedule for the Faculty of Science.
Please note that the schedule is different from that of the fourth semester of the first semester of the College of Liberal Arts and the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology.

Summer

S Semester : April 5 (Wed.) - July 31 (Mon.)

Examination and make-up class period : July 25 (Tue.) - July 31 (Mon.)

S1 Term : April 5 (Wed.) - June 2 (Fri.)

Examination and make-up class period : May 29 (Mon.) AM, May 30 (Tue.) AM, June 1 (Thu.) - June 2 (Fri.)

S2 Term : June 5 (Mon.) - July 31 (Mon.)

Examination and make-up class period : July 25 (Tue.) - July 31 (Mon.)

Summer Break : August 1 (Tue.) - September 30 (Sat.)

Classes are suspended for the entire day on April 12 (Wed.) (The University of Tokyo Commemoration Day (Entrance Ceremony))

Afternoon classes will be cancelled on May 22 (Mon.) (Faculty of Science Welcome Party (Koishikawa Botanical Garden)) [Tentative schedule].

Afternoon classes will be cancelled on May 12 (Fri.) (May Festival (Preparation))

Friday's classes will be held on the afternoon of May 30 (Tue.)

Registration period (S semester, S1 term, S2 term): April 5 (Wed.) - April 19 (Wed.)

Winter

Semester A : October 3 (Tue.) - January 30 (Tue.)

Examination and make-up class period : January 24 (Wed.) - January 25 (Thu.), January 30 (Tue.)

A1 Term : October 3 (Tue.) - November 28 (Tue.)

Examination and make-up class period : November 22 (Wed.) - November 28 (Tue.)

A2 Term : November 29 (Wed.) - January 30 (Tue.)

Examination and make-up class period : January 24 (Wed.) - January 25 (Thu.), January 30 (Tue.)

Winter Break : December 28 (Thu.) - January 3 (Wed.)

Spring Break : January 31 (Wed.) - March 31 (Sat.)

Classes will be suspended for the whole day on November 24 (Fri.) (Komaba Festival)

Classes will be suspended on all days on January 12 (Fri.) (Common Test Preparation)

Friday's classes will be held on November 21 (Tue.)

Registration period (A semester, A1 term, A2 term): October 3 (Tue.) - October 18 (Wed.)
